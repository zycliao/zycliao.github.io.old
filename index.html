I am a Ph.D. student since Jan. 2023 at the University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Taku Komura. I obtained my M.Sc. degree at Saarland University and Max Planck Institute for Informatics (supervisor: Dr. Marc Habermann) and B.Sc. degree at Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

I had a few wonderful research internship experiences at different companies and institutes, namely Adobe Research (2021, mentor: Yang Zhou ), miHoYo (2020, mentor: Jun Xing ), MPI-INF (2019, supervisor: Gerard Pons-Moll.)

My research interests lie in the intersection of computer vision and computer graphics. More specifically, I am interested in modelling and animating digital characters driven by AI. My research goal is to develop algorithms that can free content creators from hard labours.
